# LighChat Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-07
**Applies to:** the LighChat web/PWA version (https://lighchat.app and subdomains).

## 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that a website stores in the browser. They allow remembering preferences and session state.

LighChat uses cookies and similar technologies: localStorage, sessionStorage, IndexedDB.

## 2. Cookies we use

### 2.1 Strictly necessary (always on)

Required for the Service to function. No consent required.

| Name | Purpose | Lifetime |
|------|---------|----------|
| `__session` | Firebase Auth session | Session |
| `lc_locale` | Selected UI language | 1 year |
| `lc_theme` | Light/dark theme | 1 year |
| `lc_csrf` | CSRF protection | Session |

### 2.2 Functional

Improve the user experience. On by default; can be opted out.

| Name | Purpose | Lifetime |
|------|---------|----------|
| `lc_features_welcome_v1` | Welcome overlay flag | 1 year |
| `lc_last_chat` | Last opened chat | 30 days |

### 2.3 Analytics

Used only with explicit user consent (cookie banner). Off by default.

| Name | Purpose | Lifetime |
|------|---------|----------|
| `_ga`, `_gid` | Google Analytics (if enabled) | 2 years / 1 day |
| `firebase-perf-*` | Firebase Performance Monitoring | up to 1 year |

### 2.4 Advertising

LighChat does **not** use advertising/marketing cookies.

## 3. Managing cookies

You may at any time:
- update your choice in the cookie banner;
- clear cookies via browser settings;
- use private/incognito browsing.

Disabling strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the Service.

## 4. Third-party cookies

Third-party services (Firebase, Google, payment providers when integrated) may set their own cookies under their own policies.

- Google: https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies

## 5. Changes

The Operator may update this Policy. Effective date is shown at the top of the document.
