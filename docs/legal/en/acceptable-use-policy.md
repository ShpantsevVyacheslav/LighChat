# LighChat Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Last updated:** 2026-05-07

This Policy is an integral part of the [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service).

## 1. General principle

Use LighChat responsibly, lawfully, and with respect for other users.

## 2. Strictly prohibited

### 2.1 Unlawful activity
- terrorism, extremism, incitement to violence;
- trade in drugs, weapons, humans, illegal goods;
- child sexual abuse material (CSAM);
- promotion of suicide, self-harm;
- hate speech.

### 2.2 Attacks on security
- DoS / DDoS attacks against Service infrastructure;
- exploitation of vulnerabilities outside an agreed bug bounty program;
- distribution of malware, phishing links;
- attempts to obtain unauthorized access to other users' accounts;
- circumvention of technical safeguards, protocol-level reverse engineering outside permitted cases.

### 2.3 Abuse
- spam, unsolicited mass messaging;
- automated activity without explicit bot-account labeling;
- scraping and parsing without permission;
- creation of multiple accounts to evade blocks;
- fraud, deception, impersonation.

### 2.4 Violation of others' rights
- harassment, stalking, threats;
- doxing (disclosure of third-party personal data without consent);
- distribution of non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII);
- IP infringement.

## 3. Sensitive content

Lawful 18+ content is allowed only in private and group chats between adult users with explicit consent. Strictly prohibited in:
- public channels (if introduced in future versions);
- profiles, avatars, nicknames;
- meetings with guest access.

## 4. Consequences

Violations may result in:
- content removal;
- temporary or permanent account restrictions;
- referral to law enforcement.
