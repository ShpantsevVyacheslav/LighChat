# LighChat Children Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-07
**Applies to:** all LighChat clients (web, desktop, mobile).

## 1. Age threshold

LighChat is intended for users who are **16 years of age or older**. The Service is not intended for users under 16.

## 2. Compliance

This policy is designed considering:
- 152-FZ (RF): processing of minors' data requires legal guardian consent;
- GDPR Art. 8 (EU): self-consent age — 16 (member states may lower to 13);
- COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, US): additional restrictions for users under 13;
- Russian Federal Law No. 436-FZ "On Protection of Children from Information Harmful to Their Health and Development".

## 3. Age verification

At registration the user:
- provides a date of birth;
- confirms being at least 16.

If the Operator has reasonable grounds to believe a user is under 16, it may restrict or terminate access.

## 4. What we do not do

- show ads to minors;
- use minors' data for targeted advertising;
- share minors' data with third parties for marketing.

## 5. If a user under 16 is identified

Upon parent/guardian request or self-discovery, the Operator:
- immediately blocks the account;
- deletes related data within 30 days;
- notifies the requestor of the outcome.

Requests: legal@lighchat.app with subject "Minor inquiry".

## 6. Protection from harmful content

- block and report tools (see [Content Moderation Policy](/legal/content-moderation-policy));
- ability to limit communication scope (contacts, blacklist);
- secret E2EE chats with auto-deletion;
- parental controls (planned for future versions).

## 7. Contact

Parent / guardian inquiries: **legal@lighchat.app**
